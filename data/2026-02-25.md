*Camille Labourie*

### [3] [The singularity at degenerate points in steady axisymmetric compressible free surface flows with gravity](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.20506)
*Lili Du,Chunlei Yang*

Main category: math.AP

<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究三维轴对称可压缩重力流中自由边界在退化点处的奇异性状，解决Bernoulli型拟线性自由边界问题在退化点处的分析难题，特别是缺乏Weiss型单调性公式的障碍。

Method: 首次建立了拟线性Bernoulli型自由边界问题的单调性公式，该公式在方程奇异和自由边界条件退化时都有效；建立了原点处水平平坦点的新非线性频率公式，并将其与Euler方程的补偿紧致理论相结合；系统分析了所有可能退化点处的渐近行为。

Result: 证明了远离对称轴的驻点处自由表面的唯一非平凡渐近行为是Stokes角流；非驻点轴点处自由边界可能是向下或向上的尖点；原点处只有两种可能的非平凡渐近：Garabedian的尖泡或水平平坦表面。

Conclusion: 该研究在广义可压缩三维轴对称框架下解决了Stokes猜想，建立了研究奇异Bernoulli型拟线性自由边界问题奇异性的系统方法，为Vǎrvǎrucǎ和Weiss工作的可压缩对应提供了理论基础。

Abstract: In this paper, we analyze the singular shape of the free boundary at degenerate points in a three dimensional axisymmetric compressible gravity flow. For all possible degenerate points on the free surface, we prove that the only nontrivial asymptotic behavior of the free surface at the stagnation points away from the axis of symmetry is the Stokes corner flow. The possible geometries for free boundaries at the non-stagnation axis points are downward pointing or upward pointing cusps. At the origin, there are only two nontrivial asymptotics possible: the Garabedian's pointed bubble or a horizontal flat surface.
  The problem is associated with the analysis of the degenerate points of a quasilinear free boundary problem of the Bernoulli type, and the main obstacles are the absence of a Weiss-type monotonicity formula. To achieve our goal, we establish for the first time monotonicity formulas for quasilinear Bernoulli type free boundary problems. Our formula works both when the equation becomes singular and when the free boundary condition is degenerate.
  Moreover, we establish a new nonlinear frequency formula at the horizontal flat points at the origin and integrate it with the compensated compactness theory for Euler equations, ensuring the strong convergence of variational solutions.
  Our results resolve the Stokes conjecture [Mathematical and Physical Papers, Vol. I., 1880] in a generalized compressible, three dimensional axisymmetric framework. In addition, it can also be realized as a compressible counterpart to Vǎrvǎrucǎ and Weiss [Comm. Pure Appl. Math., (67), 2014]. Our approach is completely new and gives a systematic approach for studying singularities of a singular Bernoulli type quasilinear free boundary problem.

### [6] [On the singular nature of shallow-water convergence of the intermediate long wave equation on the real line](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.20695)

Main category: math.AP

TL;DR: 该论文研究了中间长波方程(ILW)解映射的正则性，发现在浅水极限下，低频部分的解映射是解析的，而剩余部分的解映射甚至不是C²的，这解释了浅水极限中解映射正则性增益的机制。

  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究中间长波方程(ILW)解映射的正则性性质，特别是在浅水极限下解映射的行为，以理解ILW向KdV方程收敛过程中解映射正则性变化的机制。

Method: 将ILW的动力学分解为低频部分和剩余部分，分析当深度参数足够小时，低频部分解映射在L²空间中的解析性，以及剩余部分解映射的C²连续性失效。建立低频动力学向KdV方程的浅水极限收敛。

This explains the mechanism of the regularity gain of the solution map in the shallow-water limit.
